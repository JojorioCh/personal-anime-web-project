    Kinds of Anime

    Shounen

    the primary target audience alternately defined as 9 to 18 years old and as 12 to 18 years old males.

    Shounen is probably the most recognized Anime category, Thanks to the popularization of Dragonball Z, One of the most recognized Anime by many. Other big names in this genre of Anime include One piece (one of the longest running series with over 1000+ episodes), Naruto, My Hero Academia

    Click here to go to My Anime List for other Shounen which may be worth watching






    Shoujo

    Although not my favourite...

    Shoujo anime do have interesting and gripping stories. A lot of Hard Hitting Romances plot lines, but also some with action that can be comparable to Shounen, Shoujo is targeted towards a more female audience

    With this being my least knowlegeable genre, click here to head to My Anime List for a top 15 Shoujo List that may give you some ideas where to start.






    Seinen

    For the more mature (❤´艸｀❤)

    Seinen anime have some surreal stories and honestly it is one of my favourite genres. You can find psychological dramas, comedies, violence driven plots in this genre, told amazingly. Although not as popular as Shounen, Seinen definitely is my favourite genre.
